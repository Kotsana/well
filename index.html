Introduction

Welcome! This website with it's multiple pages is intended to showcase the Basotho Cultural Heritage, including clothing, music, crafts, and festivals.
This is aimed at introducing you into the Basotho Culture through images and proper definitions of the Arts and Core of the culture.
These Basotho have a long history in terms of settlement but are now settled at Lesotho, a small country land locked by South Africa, found on the far east of southern Africa.
